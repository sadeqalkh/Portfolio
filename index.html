About Sadeq Alkhoori

Sadeq Alkhoori is a young Emirati based photographer and filmmaker, he studies at New York University Abu Dhabi, majoring in Film and New Media. He possesses an undeniable love for exploring geographical sites around the world. Some include, Florence, Los Angeles, New York, Berlin and Singapore. In his course of finding himself and his voice, he hopes to portray to several people what he sees in his photography to share moments that he can't articule in words.

He held a camera since his childhood to explore his surrounding world but now, he holds a camera to seek untold narratives of reality. He believes that every challenge encountered in life assists him in learning from people and experiences. He admires exploring different disciplines in his line of study.
